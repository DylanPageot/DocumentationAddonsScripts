# In Game Commands

In addition to the dashboard interface, several chat commands are available for quick in-game interventions.

***

## `/goto [ID]`

Teleports you directly to a specific player. Replace `[ID]` with the player's in-game ID.

```
/goto 5
```

***

## `/bring [ID]`

Teleports a player to your current position. You can bring multiple players at once by separating their IDs with spaces.

```
/bring 10
/bring 10 20 30
```

***

## `/return [ID]`

Returns a player to their position before they were teleported. Useful for sending a player back after a moderation conversation without leaving them disoriented.

```
/return 10
/return 10 20 30
```
